RI Harvest Kitchen

Culinary & Job-Readiness Training

The Harvest Kitchen Project is a 15-week culinary and job-readiness training program for youth within the Division of Juvenile Corrections. Youth learn a wide variety of culinary skills, everything from preservation techniques to knife skills to sales and marketing. Youth make a line of high-quality preserved foods using ingredients sourced from local farmers, including their well-known and very popular apple sauce product.

Connecting for Children and Families is a community-based organization in Woonsocket Rhode Island dedicated to improving the lives of families in the community. This Culinary Arts Training program is one of the programs they offer to provide employment education and training to adults in the area.
