Since beginning in the spring of 2011, the John Hope/Brown partnership, “Cultivating Hope,” has expanded its network of people and organizations with every season. This graphic highlights many of the players, key to the growth of the collaboration, and visualizes the importance of community-building to a project that is sustainable, inclusive, and resilient.

Not listed but just as integral are the many teachers, students, and staff at John Hope Settlement House and Federal Hill House Association; scores of students in three semesters of “Sustenance and Sustainability,” one semester each of “Urban Agriculture,” “Community Engagement,” and “Environmental Justice”; and the individuals in the Providence community who have contributed time, energy, and resources to the project. We are grateful to you all.
