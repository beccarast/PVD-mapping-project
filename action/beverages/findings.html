SUGAR-SWEETENED BEVERAGES:
OUR FINDINGS


  • Framing the Issue
  • Our Findings
  • What's Next?

In our first year of research, we found that, on average, stores within 1/4 mile of Providence public elementary schools offered 28 different varieties of sugar-sweetened beverages. Of the hundreds of brands of SSBs, 75% are owned by just 3 companies: Coca-Cola, Pepsi, and Dr. Pepper/Snapple Group. These charts represent our research process and findings (percentages don't quite add up to 100 due to rounding).
